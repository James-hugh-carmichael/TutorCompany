Made By Students - For Students

My journey began in secondary school, where I found that being tutored by university students made all the difference. They weren’t just tutors; they were mentors who understood the challenges I faced and helped me gradually transition into university life. Inspired by their support, I decided to create a space where current university students can pass on their knowledge, making education more relatable, engaging and tailored to your needs

Our Tutors

Students are always put first that's why all our tutors are fully DBS checked, interviewed and flexible for you.

Every tutor at ForTheStudents is a current university student who has excelled in the subjects they teach. They’re not only knowledgeable, but they also understand the challenges you’re facing because they’ve been there too. They’re here to help you succeed, not just academically, but in building the confidence you need for the future.

Become a tutor

I made this site because I was frustrated of hidden fees, not being able to work on my own schedule and only getting a small amount of money that I charged when tutoring. I believe putting you first, making signing up easy and a free DBS check on us if you are successful in interview.
